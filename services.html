Let's talk Data Science!

An overview of how your business can benefit

LLMs & NLP

You know what the fuss is about these days: Large Language Models (LLMs) with ChatGPT being the most famous example.
But what do they actually do and how can your business benefit from utilizing one?

Besides creating chatbots LLMs can be used for text classification (positive/negative, True/False statement, etc.), question answering and summarization just to name a few.
This gets really handy when specializing a model with data from your business!

Machine Learning

Machine Learning (ML) can be used to solve a variety of use cases including fraud detection, customer profiling, pricing and distress modeling, just to name a few.

With the development of a model comes the end-to-end setup.
We’ll make sure that everything from data collection and preprocessing to model training, evaluation, and deployment, is taken care of.
Let’s design and optimize ML pipelines to ensure efficient model development, deployment and scalability!

Explainable Models

Certain use cases like credit rating require fully transparent and explainable models, also known as glassbox models, where the user fully understands the decision making.

We’ll ensure clear insights into why machine learning models make specific predictions.
A glassbox model can also help facilitate better decision making and build trust to users outside the data science team, in addition to being a requirement for the specific business case.

Analytics and Statistics

Many business cases can be solved with a simpler, more straightforward approach.
An overview of data with some descriptive statistics is a great first step, when data is coming from multiple sources and is not easily accessible to the team.

Enabling faster and more informed decision making is important for high performing teams!

Data Integration & Pipelines

Starting from scratch on a new project? Let’s get the dataflow going!

We’ll start by extracting all data needed for the project and setting up the data structure, then transform and combine it into a useful format. This will be the foundation for doing analytics and ML!
We’ll design the automated workflow for continuous updates and quality checks.
