Maersk: Development and maintenance of capacity control software along with scoping and development of a reinforcement learning (RL) based approach to continuous pricing of containers in the spot market.

Risika: Development of credit risk models along with the explanability thereof.
Responsible for the entire ML pipeline including data engineering and feature store, development and productionization of models, pipeline & workflow setup, containerization and explanability modules.

On project involved launched a new fully explainable “scorecard” model in three countries that greatly outperformed the previous models (AUC went from 0.75 to 0.85).

Longship Invest: Development, launch, and maintenance of an algorithmic trading strategy that beat the market (74% return vs 10% for BTC in the same period) over the three years from its initial launch until leaving the project in Nov 2023.

The setup includes monitoring and database integrations, along with a dashboard setup to track investments.

PinPoint Tracking: Development of a prototype for a standalone tracking IoT device using satellite communications.
This includes hardware assembly and component specifications, programming of device software and backend systems and testing/validation of the product.

The prototype contributed to getting funding from the Danish Maritime Fund.
